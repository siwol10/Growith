Past Projects

Campus Startup Club ZERO

Our team members came together with a common problem of lacking a proper club during our studies. This gathering gradually developed into a large club with 70 members, evolving beyond a simple meeting to become a startup club undertaking various projects. These experiences have laid the foundation for collaboration among team members and the generation of innovative ideas.

Car Stopper Development for Quick Parking

In 2023, through a project organized by the Ministry of Science and ICT, our team developed a car stopper with an integrated location-detecting camera. This technology optimizes the use of parking spaces and significantly enhances user convenience. The project’s results were scientifically validated and eventually published in a paper that won a gold award, officially recognizing Growith’s technological prowess and innovative approach.

Autonomous Park Environment Management Mobility

This project will be conducted at the mobility camp hosted by the Academy. We aim to develop an autonomous park environment management mobility system based on user requests. This system will automate park maintenance tasks, ensuring cleanliness and providing a pleasant environment for visitors.

Planning and App Development for University Student Contests and External Activities

We will plan contests and external activities for university students and develop a platform app to manage them. This platform will provide information on various contests and external opportunities, making it easier for students to participate.
