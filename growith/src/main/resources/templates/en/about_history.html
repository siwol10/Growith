Company History

Background of Establishment

Growit is a startup founded to solve various problems and create a sustainable future, embodying the meaning of "growing together." It began with passionate individuals met during university life coming together.

Initial Motivation:
The founder of Growit, CEO Myung-jun Kang, participated in various projects during university to realize creative ideas. He became aware of the seriousness of water pollution and gained experience by participating in various activities to address it. During this process, he developed a desire to create a lab where students could independently conduct research and experiments.

Growth Process:
Since its establishment, Growit has continuously grown. Initially focused on an autonomous fountain system for water quality improvement, our goal is not limited to solving a single problem. We are conducting various projects to address different environmental issues and bring about positive change in society.

Future Vision:
Based on the philosophy of "growing together," Growit will continue to create a better future through innovative technologies and ideas. Currently focused on the PaDO project for water quality improvement, we plan to undertake various projects to solve diverse environmental issues and build a sustainable society.

Growit, founded on the passion and creativity of university students, aims to independently research, experiment, and solve various problems. We will continue to grow through various projects and collaborations, making a positive impact on society.
