PaDO

PaDO is an autonomous fountain system focused on preventing and removing algal blooms. It is an innovative solution that detects algae early through sensors and data analysis, improves water quality in an environmentally friendly way, and enhances the city's appearance.

PaDO

Key Technologies & Advantages

Improved Water Circulation

Improves water circulation, a major cause of algae blooms.

Water Quality Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of water quality through various sensors.

Early Warning

Early warnings and preventive measures for algae blooms.

Autonomous Fountain

Automation of autonomous fountains for accurate and quick algae removal.

Cost Reduction

Reduces labor and costs by incorporating various functions with in-house technology.

Aesthetic Improvement

Provides suitable aesthetics through fountain functionality and environmental adaptation.

Customizable Design

Customizable design and LED lighting systems according to usage.

Eco-Friendly Technology

Environmentally friendly technology using natural water circulation while minimizing chemical use.
